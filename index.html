FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How does the MediPoints rewards system work?

expand_more
MediPoints are earned for every completed shift, perfect rating, referral, and survey response. 100 MediPoints = $1.00 in cashback. Points can be redeemed for direct deposit, gift cards, or healthcare benefits. Your balance never expires.

Is MediSync HIPAA compliant?

expand_more
Yes. MediSync is fully HIPAA compliant and SOC 2 Type II certified. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit. We conduct annual third-party security audits and maintain comprehensive audit logs for all system access.

How do I get paid for referrals?

expand_more
Referral bonuses are triggered when your referred colleague completes their first shift. The $500 bonus is deposited directly to your connected bank account within 5 business days. There is no cap on referrals.

What credentials do I need to join as a caregiver?

expand_more
Requirements vary by shift type. RN and LPN positions require a valid state license and current CPR/BLS certification. CNA positions require state certification. All caregivers must pass a background check and provide proof of immunization records.

Can I work at multiple facilities?

expand_more
Absolutely. MediSync is designed for flexibility. You can be credentialed at multiple facilities simultaneously and pick up shifts based on preference, location, and pay rate. There's no minimum shift requirement.
